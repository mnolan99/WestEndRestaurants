Frequently Asked Questions

What is West End Restaurants?

We are a website aimed mainly at students of the University of Glasgow highlighting the best places to eat in Glasgow's West End - You can read reviews as well as make them and find the best place for you.

I can't find my favourite restaurant/cafe to review - how can I create a review?

Use our contact us page to request restaurants to add to our website, we want to make sure all the best places are right here - so don't hesitate to write to us.

I own a restaurant/cafe in the West End and I want to offer deals on your site. How can I do that?

Just send us a request through our contact us page! We can't wait to hear from you.
